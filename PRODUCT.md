# Product Context

## Register
brand

## Product Purpose
Turn the April 27, 2026 meeting on AI privacy, model choice, context, validation, and assessment into a polished faculty-facing web guide.

## Users
Bar-Ilan faculty, clinicians, researchers, and students who need practical AI guidance without technical jargon.

## Tone
Calm, editorial, rigorous, and useful. The page should feel like an academic magazine feature, not a generic AI startup page.

## Strategic Principles
- Privacy comes before productivity when data is sensitive.
- Model choice should follow the task, not brand loyalty.
- Good AI work depends on context, output specifications, and validation.
- Assessment should prove understanding rather than pretending AI does not exist.

## Anti-References
- Dropdown-heavy navigation.
- Self-explaining section copy that tells visitors how the page is designed.
- Abstract circles, squares, glowing tech loops, and generic AI motifs.
- Long repetitive card stacks.
- Side-stripe card accents.
- Underlined links.
